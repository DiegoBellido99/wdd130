Sandboard

Huacachina Sandboarding one of the most incredible adventure activities in Ica. Dare and slide on the dunes of Ica in the Peruvian desert. In addition, when sandboarding Ica you will be able to see the famous Huacachina lagoon, one of the most incredible attractions in Peru.

Huacachina is interconnected with the Panamericana Sur at a height of 406 m. s. n. m. It is eminently coastal despite having the Andes mountain range, it has a warm desert climate with an annual temperature of 22°C, strong winds are normal and common during the summer months, it has a great influence of tourists, one of its most attractive features. visited is; Huacachina, five kilometers to the west, also known as the Huacachina Oasis, is a lagoon hidden among the dunes of the desert surrounded by palm trees.

    Recommendations:
  • Wear light, comfortable clothing, avoiding cotton clothing
  • Don't forget to apply sunscreen before heading out into the desert.

Surfing

Surfing is an adventure sport that involves sliding and making turns on a wave while standing on a board. Peru is classified as the cradle of surfing. one of the busiest places in Latin America to practice this sport and is home to many championships, one of the most important in the National Board Championship, which already has more than 30 versions.

At the beginning of the 20th century, Carlos Dogny Larco, founder of the surfing scene in Peru, promoted the development of this adventure sport including all of South America, taking as its main headquarters the beaches (Makaha and Waikiki) of the Terrazas, Waikiki and Pacífico clubs. South on the Balta descent.

The preferred place to practice this sport by surfing lovers are the beaches “Punta Roquitas” and “Makaha”, they are perfect since the waves are rougher than on other beaches within the 5 kilometers that comprise them. Additionally, if you are a beginner you can find academies and/or classes that are taught around the beach to learn to surf.

    Recommendations:
  • You must know how to swim.
