Nazca lines

The Nazca lines and Jumana pampas geoglyphs or simply Nazca or Nasca lines are ancient geoglyphs found in the Jumana pampas, in the Nazca desert, between the towns of Nazca and Palpa, in the department of Ica (Peru). They were drawn by the Nazca culture and are composed of several hundred figures that range from designs as simple as lines, to complex zoomorphic, phytomorphic and geometric figures that appear drawn on the earth's surface. Since 1994, the UNESCO Committee has registered the lines and geoglyphs of Nazca and Pampas de Jumana as a World Heritage Site. You can visit this place by boarding a small plane to see all the figures from the sky, there are 800 figures!!!

This archaeological site is located in the region called Ica, during your visit you can eat the typical foods which are:

  • Carapulcra
  • Sopa seca
  • Frejol colado
  • tejas

Vinicunca

This is the mountain of Seven Colors, also known as Vinicunca or Rainbow. The mountain is located in the Vilcanota Mountain Range at 5,200 meters above sea level, in the district of Pitumarca. Its slopes and peaks are dyed by stripes of intense shades of fuchsia, turquoise, lavender and gold.

In August 2017, it appeared on Business Insider's list of 100 places to visit before you die recommended by travel experts.

If you leave the city of Cusco, you must head towards the South Valley, for about two hours until you reach Cusipata. From there you must go to the community of Phulawasipata from where you can begin the ascent on foot. This community is located more than 4000 meters above sea level, so it is recommended that you take it easy and walk at a gentle and sustained pace. Remember that you are going to climb more than 5000 meters to reach your destination. .

The walk takes approximately 1:30 to 2 hours and along the way you can see some animals in the area such as llamas, alpacas and vicuñas and enjoy the high Andean landscape full of ichu everywhere. You will be able to share with local people and talk with them while you learn more about their customs and traditions.This beautiful place is located in Cusco, so you could take advantage of trying the typical food:

  • Sopa de quinua
  • Cuy al horno
  • Choclo con queso
  • Chicharron cuzqueño
